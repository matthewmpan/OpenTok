# OpenTok

OpenTok is a Safari extension that allows you to view TikTok without requiring an app download.

## Features

- View TikTok videos directly in Safari.
- No need to download the TikTok app.

## Installation

To install OpenTok on your iPhone, follow these steps:

1. Download the OpenTok project from GitHub.
2. Open the project in Xcode.
3. Connect your iPhone to your Mac.
4. In Xcode, select your iPhone as the target device and click the "Run" button.
5. Once the app is installed, go to Settings > Safari > Content Blockers on your iPhone.
6. Enable OpenTok.

## Setting Up Permissions

OpenTok needs certain permissions to function correctly. To set up these permissions:

1. Go to Settings > Safari > Content Blockers on your iPhone.
2. Enable OpenTok.
3. Go back to Settings > Safari and scroll down to the "Privacy & Security" section.
4. Enable "Allow Cross-Website Tracking".

## Usage

After installing OpenTok, you can view TikTok videos directly in Safari. Just navigate to a TikTok URL and the video will play automatically.
